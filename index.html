Methodology

How We Verify Every Data Point

Our 4-step process ensures you get reliable numbers, not recycled forum speculation.

01

Source from OEM

Every spec table begins with official service manuals, TSBs, and manufacturer datasheets — the primary source of truth.

02

Cross-check Community Data

Forum reports from PriusChat, r/EcoFlow, and RCGroups are collected and statistically filtered to confirm or flag OEM claims.

03

Lab Standard Alignment

Values are aligned to ISO 12405 (Li-ion traction), IEC 62133, and OSHA 1910.269 safety standards as applicable.

04

Date-Stamped Verification

Each page carries a "Last Verified" date. Data older than 6 months is flagged for re-verification before it's shown as current.
