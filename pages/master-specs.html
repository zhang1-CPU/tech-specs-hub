Battery Chemistry Quick Reference

LFPLithium Iron Phosphate
  • 3,0005,000+ cycles to 80% capacity
  • Thermally stable - no thermal runaway under normal conditions
  • Lower energy density means heavier units for same capacity
  • Recommended for stationary and frequent-use applications
NMCNickel Manganese Cobalt
  • Higher energy density - lighter for equivalent capacity
  • ~800 cycles before significant degradation
  • Higher risk of thermal runaway at high temperatures
  • Suitable for portable/travel use where weight matters most
