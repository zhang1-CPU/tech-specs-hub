Privacy Policy

What we track, what we don't, cookie usage, and your data rights.

The short version

We use basic analytics (Google Analytics 4) to understand which pages are useful. We don't sell data. We don't have user accounts. We use standard cookies for site functionality. That's really it.

The rest of this page is the formal version required for GDPR and CCPA compliance, but if you only read one section, you've read it.

01.Information We Collect

We collect two types of information on TechSpecsHub:

  • Server logs — Standard web server log entries including your IP address, browser user-agent string, the page you requested, and a timestamp. These are kept by our CDN provider (Cloudflare) for security and abuse detection. They're rotated automatically.
  • Aggregate analytics — Pageviews, approximate geographic region (country-level), referrer, and device type, processed through Google Analytics 4. No personally identifiable information is sent by us. Google may collect additional data according to their own privacy policy.

We don't have user accounts. We don't run ad networks. We don't ask for your email address unless you choose to contact us.

02.Cookies and Similar Technologies

We use two categories of cookies:

  • Session cookies — Short-lived, first-party cookies for basic site functionality (remembering a modal was dismissed). These are not used for tracking.
  • GA4 cookies — Cookies set by Google Analytics 4 for pageview counting and anonymous aggregate reporting. See "Third-party services" below for the GA4 opt-out plugin.

03.How We Use Information

Every piece of information we collect has one purpose only:

  • Content improvement — Which pages are actually read, which error codes people search for, which device categories are in demand. This directly shapes what we publish next.
  • Error fixing — Broken internal links, pages that load slowly, browsers we don't officially support. Aggregate analytics help us spot these.
  • Aggregate traffic analysis only — We never attempt to identify individual users, and we don't store user-level analytics data.

04.Third-Party Services

  • Google Analytics 4 — Used for aggregate traffic and page performance analytics. Subject to Google's privacy policy.
  • Cloudflare — Hosting, CDN, DDoS protection, and content delivery. Cloudflare processes IP addresses and may set cookies for security purposes. Subject to Cloudflare's privacy policy.

05.Data Retention

Google Analytics 4 data retention on our property is set to 14 months. Data older than 14 months is automatically deleted by Google. We don't export or archive raw user-level analytics data.

06.Your Rights (GDPR / CCPA)

Depending on your location (EU/EEA or California), you have the right to access, correct, delete, restrict, or export personal data held about you. You also have the right to object to certain types of processing.

Because we don't hold user accounts and don't associate analytics data with individual identities, a typical "access my data" request will return a confirmation that we hold no personally identifiable record on you.

How to opt out of Google Analytics

Google provides an official browser plugin that blocks Analytics tracking across all sites. Install it and it will handle opt-out automatically:

Install Google Analytics Opt-out

You can also set your browser to reject all third-party cookies, or enable "Do Not Track" (DNT) in your browser settings.

07.Children's Privacy

TechSpecsHub is not directed at children under 13 and we don't knowingly collect information from them. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information (for example, by emailing us), contact us and we'll delete it promptly.

08.Changes to This Policy

When we change this policy we'll update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. For substantive changes (new data category, new third-party service), we'll also add a notice on the site homepage for 30 days.

09.Contact Information

For any privacy-related question, request, or complaint, email us at:

hello@powerspecshub.com

We respond to privacy inquiries within 14 business days.
