Conversion Tips

Why Voltage Matters for Wh to mAh

Watt-hours = Volts × Amp-hours. Same mAh at different voltages = different Wh. Always check both specs!

Metric Prefixes

1 kWh = 1,000 Wh | 1 MWh = 1,000,000 Wh | 1 mAh = 0.001 Ah

US vs Metric Units

Miles, pounds, Fahrenheit - US uses different systems. Use these converters to compare specs accurately.
