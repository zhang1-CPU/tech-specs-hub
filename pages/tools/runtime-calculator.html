Tips for Accurate Calculations

Understand Peak vs. Continuous

Some appliances (like microwaves) draw more power at startup. Use the continuous wattage for estimation.

Battery Age Matters

New batteries deliver full capacity. After 500+ cycles, expect 80-90% of rated capacity.

Temperature Effects

Cold weather reduces capacity by 10-20%. Optimal operating temp: 15-25°C (597-7°F).

Reserve Capacity

Most power stations protect 5-10% battery. Actual usable capacity = rated × 90-95%.
