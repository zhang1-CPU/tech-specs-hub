Never calibrate the compass indoors or near vehicles. Incorrect calibration leads to flyaways and crashes.

What is Compass Error?

A Compass Error occurs when the drone's magnetometer detects abnormal magnetic fields or inconsistent heading data. The compass works alongside the IMU to determine orientation and is critical for stable flight and return-to-home accuracy. Errors are typically caused by flying near large metal structures, power lines, reinforced concrete, or carrying magnetic accessories. In some cases, the compass itself may be miscalibrated or failing. Most drones will refuse to take off if a compass error is active, while others may switch to ATTI mode and drift. Calibration is a simple procedure that involves rotating the drone along its axes to map the local magnetic field. It should be performed away from vehicles, buildings, and metal objects.

Common Symptoms

  • App shows 'Compass Error' or 'Magnetic Interference'
  • Drone refuses to arm or enter GPS mode
  • Drone yaws or drifts without stick input
  • Return-to-home trajectory is inaccurate
  • Calibration prompt appears repeatedly

Tools You'll Need

  • DJI Fly / DJI GO 4 / manufacturer app
  • Open area away from metal and electronics

Step-by-Step Fix

  1. 01
    Move at least 50 meters away from cars, buildings, power lines, and reinforced concrete.
  2. 02
    Power on the drone and remote, connect to the app.
  3. 03
    Navigate to Settings > Safety > Compass Calibration.
  4. 04
    Hold the drone horizontally and rotate it 360 degrees until the app beeps or lights turn green.
  5. 05
    Tilt the drone vertically (nose down) and rotate 360 degrees again until confirmation.
  6. 06
    If calibration fails, move to a different location and check for magnetic accessories attached to the drone.
  7. 07
    After calibration, verify the compass heading in the app matches real-world orientation.
  8. 08
    If errors persist in multiple locations, the compass module may need replacement.

When to Call a Professional

If calibration fails in multiple open areas and no magnetic accessories are attached, the internal compass module may be defective.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to calibrate before every flight?
No. Calibrate when moving to a new region, after hard impacts, or when the app prompts you.
Can a phone case cause compass errors?
Yes, if the case contains magnets or metal. Remove it from the drone or use a non-magnetic mount.
Why does my drone spin in circles?
Severe compass interference causes yaw drift. Land immediately and recalibrate in a safe area.
