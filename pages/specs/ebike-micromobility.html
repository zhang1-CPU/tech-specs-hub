Common Error Codes

CodeDescriptionPossible CauseSolution
05 Motor Hall Sensor Error Loose connection, damaged Hall sensor Check 3-pin motor connector, replace motor if damaged
06 Low Voltage Protection Battery depleted, bad connection Charge battery, check connector pins
07 Motor Overheat Excessive load, climbing steep hills Let motor cool 15-20 min, reduce load
08 Throttle Signal Error Thumb throttle malfunction Check throttle connector, replace if damaged
09 Brake Signal Error E-brake sensor disengaged Check brake lever sensors, reconnect or replace
10 Controller Overheat High ambient temp, excessive power draw Let cool, ensure adequate ventilation
21 Speed Sensor Error Magnet displaced, sensor dirty Reposition spoke magnet, clean sensor
25 Cruise Mode Error Throttle engaged while entering cruise Release throttle fully before activating cruise

Routine Maintenance for Bafang Motors

TaskFrequencyHow To Do It
Check Connectors Weekly Inspect motor, battery, and display connectors for corrosion or loose pins
Lubricate Chain Every 200 km Clean chain with degreaser, apply e-bike specific lubricant
Torque Bolts Monthly Check motor mount bolts, crank arms, and chainring bolts for proper torque
Clean Speed Sensor Monthly Wipe magnet and sensor with isopropyl alcohol, ensure proper gap

Battery Care & Storage Tips

Storage Temperature: Store battery at 15?5°C, never below freezing or above 40°C

Charge Level: Store at 40?0% charge, never fully charged or fully discharged

Long-Term Storage ( > 1 month): Check and recharge to 50% every 2 months

Water Protection: Never submerge battery, avoid riding in heavy rain without proper sealing

Scooter Maintenance Schedule

TaskFrequencyHow To Do It
Check Tire Pressure Every ride Inflate to recommended PSI (usually 35-50 PSI depending on model)
Clean Deck & Components Weekly Wipe with damp cloth, avoid spraying water directly on electronics
Check Stem & Folding Mechanism Monthly Tighten bolts, lubricate folding hinge with silicone spray
Inspect Brakes Every 500 km Check brake pad wear, adjust cable tension if needed

Scooter Battery Best Practices

Avoid Deep Discharges: Don't let battery go below 20% regularly

Charge After Rides: Charge within 24 hours of riding, especially after heavy use

Temperature Awareness: Don't charge in temperatures below 0°C or above 40°C

Water Protection: Never use pressure washers, wipe only with damp cloth
