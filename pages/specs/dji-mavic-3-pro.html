Battery Safety Notes

  • High Voltage: 17.6V full charge. Never short circuit the battery terminals.
  • Storage: Store at 3.8-3.9V/cell (15.2-15.6V pack). Never store fully charged or depleted.
  • Travel: Under 100Wh - allowed in carry-on luggage (check airline regulations).
  • Disposal: LiPo batteries must be discharged to 0% and recycled at proper facilities.

Intelligent Flight Modes

MasterShots

Automated cinematic shots. DJI selects optimal flight path and gimbal movement based on subject type and distance.

ActiveTrack 360°

Tracks subject while flying around it in 360-degree circle. Can maintain subject framing regardless of flight direction.

Waypoints

Plan custom flight routes. Mark waypoints on map. Drone automatically flies to each point while maintaining camera settings.

Cruise Control

Maintain fixed heading and speed. Free up attention for camera composition and gimbal control.

Return to Home (RTH)

Automatically calculates optimal return path. Uses obstacle sensing to avoid obstacles during return flight.

Spotlight 2.0

Camera automatically locks onto subject. Pilot controls position while gimbal maintains focus on subject.
