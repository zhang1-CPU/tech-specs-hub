Battery Care & Optimization

Charge Regularly: Don't let battery drop below 20% before charging

Avoid Extreme Temperatures: Keep watch between 0°C and 45°C for best performance

Long-Term Storage: Store with battery at 40?0% charge

Save Battery: Turn off unused features (music, always-on display) when not needed
