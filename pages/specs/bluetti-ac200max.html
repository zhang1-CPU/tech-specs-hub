Warranty 2 years (4 years with registration)

Common Troubleshooting

Expansion Battery Not Detected

  1. Turn off AC200MAX completely
  2. Disconnect and reconnect the expansion cable firmly
  3. Check if cable is undamaged (look for bent pins)
  4. Power on AC200MAX and wait 10 seconds
  5. If still not detected, try a different expansion battery

Slow AC Charging

  1. Standard AC adapter: 500W (~4 hours full charge)
  2. With charging brick (sold separately): 1,200W (~2 hours)
  3. Verify outlet provides 120V (US) or 230V (EU)
  4. Try different outlet on separate circuit

AC Output Trips Frequently

  1. Check if connected devices exceed 2,200W continuous
  2. Some devices with high inrush current may trip even below rating
  3. Try plugging devices in one at a time to isolate culprit
  4. Ensure no damaged plugs or cables
