Battery Care Tips (Important!)

Avoid Full Discharges: Don't let battery run completely flat regularly ?this reduces lifespan

Charge Temperature: Charge at room temperature (15?5°C), avoid extreme cold/heat

Storage: If storing for >1 month, charge to ~50% and remove battery from vacuum

Yellow Light: When yellow light appears, charge immediately ?don't continue using

Suction Loss Troubleshooting

1 Check Filter First

Washable HEPA filter clogging is the #1 cause of suction loss. Remove, rinse, and ensure completely dry before reinstalling.

2 Inspect Airway Blockages

Check wand, floor head, and bin inlet for debris. Use a pipe cleaner or compressed air to clear obstructions.

3 Verify Brush Bar Rotation

If brush bar isn't spinning, remove hair tangle, check end caps for damage, and ensure it's seated correctly.
