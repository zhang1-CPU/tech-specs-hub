P0A80 Diagnostic Flowchart

Follow this sequence when P0A80 (Replace Hybrid Battery Pack) is stored in the ECU. Do not replace the entire pack until completing module-level isolation.

flowchart TD A([P0A80 Retrieved via OBD-II]) --> B{Check for companion codes\nP0A7F / P0A7E / P3000?} B -- Yes --> C[Clear all codes\nRoad-test 15 min\nmonitor HV voltage spread] B -- No --> D[Pull HV service plug\nWait 10 min\nVerify <50VDC] C --> E{Codes return?} E -- No --> F([Monitor only\nPossible transient BMS fault]) E -- Yes --> D D --> G[Measure each module\ninternal resistance\nwith battery analyzer] G --> H{Any module >40mΩ\nor voltage <6.5V?} H -- No --> I[Check BMS wiring harness\nand temperature sensors\nContact dealer] H -- Yes --> J[Replace failed modules\nBalance remaining modules\nReassemble with correct torque] J --> K[Reinstall HV service plug\nClear P0A80 via Techstream\nRoad-test 20+ min] K --> L{P0A80 returns?} L -- No --> M([Repair complete ✓]) L -- Yes --> N([Replace full pack\nor remaining bad modules])

Diagnostic tool required: Toyota Techstream or compatible (e.g., Autel MaxiSys, Snap-on ZEUS). Generic OBD-II readers may retrieve P0A80 but cannot display HV module voltage block data.
