Key Differentiators

Split-Phase 120/240V

Native 120/240V split-phase output eliminates the need for external transformers. Powers 240V appliances like dryers, well pumps, and EV chargers directly. [S1]

Ultra-Quiet Operation

Noise level of ≤30 dB under 2,000W load — quieter than a library (40 dB). Suitable for indoor and nighttime use without disturbance. [S1]

EV-Grade LFP Cells

LFP (LiFePO4) chemistry with EV-grade cells delivers 4,000 cycles to 80% capacity — approximately 10+ years of daily use. Superior thermal stability and safety vs NMC. [S1]

10ms UPS Switch

10ms EPS (Emergency Power Supply) switch time protects sensitive electronics like desktop PCs, NAS devices, and servers from power interruptions. [S1]

OASIS Smart Home

EcoFlow OASIS integration supports Matter, Thread, and Shelly protocols for seamless smart home automation. Panel 2 provides 12-circuit management with 20ms switch-over. [S1]

Smart Generator 4000

Dual-fuel (gasoline/LPG) generator with auto start/stop. DC direct charging at 3,200W eliminates AC conversion losses. Integrates with EcoFlow App for remote monitoring. [S1]
