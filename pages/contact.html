What to Include in Your Message

We reply to most messages in 2-3 business days. Including the information below drastically speeds up our investigation and ensures you actually get a useful response.

1

Device brand and exact model number

E.g., "EcoFlow Delta Pro 3" (not just "EcoFlow power station"). Model number matters — sometimes several revisions share a brand name.

2

The specific spec or error code

E.g., "What's the idle drain in watts?" or "I'm getting Error E03 on startup and the manual doesn't explain it."

3

What you've already tried

For troubleshooting questions — "I've tried a hard reset, different power outlets, and the firmware updater tool." The more detail, the less back-and-forth we need.

4

Source links if you have them

Linking to a manual, forum thread, manufacturer page, or spec sheet helps us verify. It's not required, but it speeds up our investigation and our response.

Response time

We personally read every message. Response time is 2-3 business days for most inquiries; longer for detailed spec research requests on devices that aren't already in our database.
