What to Include in Your Message

1

Brand and model

The exact device name, including revision or generation suffix if applicable.

2

The spec or error code

Quote the number, code, or sentence that you're reporting. Copy-paste from our page if possible.

3

What you've already tried

If reporting a troubleshooting issue, list the steps you've already taken to diagnose it.

4

A source, if you have one

A link to a datasheet, manual, or measurement. This dramatically speeds up review time.
